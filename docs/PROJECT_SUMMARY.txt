🎯 PROJECT OVERVIEW

This is a complete IoT monitoring system designed to:
• Track real-time viewing distance to laptop/monitor screens
• Prevent Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS) and eye strain
• Provide instant alerts when posture/distance is suboptimal
• Store historical data for trend analysis
• Support personalized thresholds and notifications

⚕️ HEALTH STANDARDS (Clinical Reference)

SAFE VIEWING DISTANCE
├─ Recommended: 62-75 cm (optimal for monitor)
├─ Minimum: 40 cm (from clinical guidelines)
└─ Maximum: 100 cm (to avoid straining eyes)

CVS RISK FACTORS
├─ Distance < 30 cm: HIGH RISK (danger zone)
├─ Distance 30-40 cm: ELEVATED RISK (warning zone)
└─ Distance ≥ 62 cm: LOW RISK (safe zone)

RECOMMENDED BREAKS
├─ 20-20-20 Rule: Every 20 min, look 20 feet away for 20 sec
├─ Blinking rate: Increase from 17 to 20+ times per minute
├─ Lighting: Maintain 300-500 lux (well-lit environment)
└─ Posture: Monitor position, keep neutral spine
