🛠️ Calibration Tool

Adjust the ultrasonic sensor's zero point to match your ergonomic sitting posture.

Step 1: Position

Sit upright, make sure your eyes are level with the screen. Keep an ideal viewing distance (around 62-65cm).

Step 2: Measure

The system will take 10 data samples (Moving Average) to find the most stable distance point.

Step 3: Save

The measurement result will be saved as your personal threshold limit.
